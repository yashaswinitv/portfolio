Tower of Hanoi

The Tower of Hanoi is a classic algorithmic puzzle where disks of different sizes must be moved from a source peg to a destination peg following these rules: only one disk moves at a time, and a larger disk cannot be placed on a smaller one.

Timeline
Jan 2023 – Apr 2023
Role
Undergraduate Researcher
Stack
python, c, c++, matlab
Contributions
Provides step-by-step solution tracking for educational purposes.

Approach

  • Recursive Algorithm – Uses recursion to move disks step by step following the rules.
  • Iterative Algorithm – Solves the puzzle using loops and stack data structures without recursion.
  • Graphical Simulation – Visualizes each move on a GUI for better understanding.
  • Frame-Stewart Algorithm – Optimized approach for solving Tower of Hanoi with more than 3 pegs.

Highlights

  • Demonstrates recursion and problem-solving strategies effectively.
  • Calculates minimum moves and tracks each disk transfer.
